Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new task?

+

Simply type your task in the input field and click "Add Task" or press Enter on your keyboard. Your task will be instantly added to the list.

How do I mark a task as complete?

+

Click the checkbox next to any task to mark it as complete. The task will be crossed out and counted in your completed statistics. Click again to mark it as incomplete.

Can I delete a task?

+

Yes! Click the "Delete" button on any task to remove it permanently from your list.

Are my tasks saved automatically?

+

Yes, all your tasks are automatically saved to your browser's local storage. Your tasks will persist even if you close the browser or refresh the page.

How do I filter tasks?

+

Use the filter buttons to view "All" tasks, only "Pending" tasks, or only "Completed" tasks. This helps you focus on what you need to work on.

Is my data private?

+

Absolutely! All your tasks are stored locally on your device. We don't have access to your data, and nothing is sent to any server.

Does TaskFlow work offline?

+

Yes! Once you've loaded TaskFlow, it works completely offline. Your tasks are stored locally, so you don't need an internet connection to use the app.

Can I use TaskFlow on mobile?

+

Yes! TaskFlow is fully responsive and works great on smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers.

Is there a limit to how many tasks I can create?

+

No limits! Create as many tasks as you need. The only limitation is your browser's local storage capacity, which is typically more than enough for thousands of tasks.

How do I clear all my tasks?

+

Currently, you can delete tasks one by one using the Delete button. If you want to clear all data, you can clear your browser's local storage for this site.
